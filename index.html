    Frequently Asked Questions for Personal Loans

    • Is collateral/security required for an instant personal loan from Money Market Solution?

      • Money Market Solution instant loans are unsecured loans, hence you do not have to provide collateral or security of any sort.

    • What documents do I need to get an instant personal loan from Money Market Solution?

      • Our documentation requirements are minimal. We will only require the following:

      • Your PAN number
      • Your mobile number will have to be linked to your Aadhaar card for KYC verification
      • Clear selfie taken in a well-lit area
      • Please note that we may require proof of address as well as a bank statement based on your profile.
    • Is it safe to get an instant personal loan online from Money Market Solution?

      • Money Market Solution’s security systems are designed to be in line with those of the best banks in the country. You can be rest assured knowing that all your data is safe and secure as we use 256-bit data encryption for data management.

    • What happens to my existing instant loan if I avail a top-up loan from Money Market Solution?

      • When you take a top-up loan, we generate a new loan account for you. This new instant loan account has the due amount of the existing loan along with the top-up loan amount.

    • What are the applicable interest rates for my instant personal loan from Money Market Solution?

      • On your screen, you should be able to see a column called 'Monthly EMI'. That is the amount you will be paying each month. The Loan term column tells you the duration of the loan, and you will have to pay your 'Monthly EMI' every month for that duration. The interest column is for your information only, and specifies the entire interest you are paying on that loan amount in the entire duration. It is not related to the amount you will be paying.

    • Will there be a credit report inquiry when I apply for an instant loan from Money Market Solution?

      • Yes, there will be a credit report enquiry made whenever you avail an instant loan from Money Market Solution. This enquiry will only be made after you submit the loan application.
